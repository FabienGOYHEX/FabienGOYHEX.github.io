For over a thousand generations, the Jedi Knights were the guardians of peace and justice in the Old Republic" -Obi-Wan Kenobi. A Jedi was a Force-sensitive individual, most often a member of the Jedi Order, who served and studied the Bright Side of the Force. The weapon of the Jedi was the lightsaber, a sword whose blade was made of pure energy. The Jedi fought for peace and justice in the Galactic Republic, most often against the Sith, mortal enemies who studied the Dark Side. The origins of the Jedi were very old and dated back more than a thousand generations. When the Clone Wars broke out, the Jedi Order had nearly 10,000 Jedi in its ranks. At the end of the conflict, however, they were decimated by the Sith during the execution of Order 66. The few survivors were hunted down and eliminated by the Inquisitorius. At the end of the Galactic Civil War, the only known living Jedi was Luke Skywalker. Skywalker embarked on the reconstruction of the order in the years following the end of the Galactic Civil War. However, his nephew and apprentice Ben Solo, who later called himself Kylo Ren, massacred all his members. Skywalker was once again the last Jedi of the galaxy. The Sith, also known as the Sith Order, was an old order of Force fighters dedicated to the dark side of the Force. Emerging as a divergent faction of the Jedi order, the Sith became the mortal enemies of the Jedi and fought them in many wars. Driven by emotions such as hatred, anger and greed, the Sith were misleading and obsessed with gaining power, no matter the price.
